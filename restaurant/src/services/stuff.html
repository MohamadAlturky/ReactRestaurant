What is a ZIP file?

ZIP files are also known as "archive" files. They use lossless compression to reduce the file size of the files contained inside the ZIP.

Thus, the ZIP file functions as a folder that groups files and uses compression to make it easier to store, send, and share their content.

How to compress to ZIP?

  1. Upload your files. They can be images, videos, documents or even a mix.
  2. Click on "Start" to begin with the ZIP compression.
